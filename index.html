Welcome to Bridgeway Church

Here at Bridgeway Church, our vision is to see our world transformed by the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Our mission is to lead people to full maturity in Jesus Christ.

About

This is our community

We believe that a person moves from being a new believer towards full maturity in Christ ultimately by the power and leading of the Holy Spirit. We also believe that maturity requires certain spiritual disciplines, and happens through a process of connecting, growing, and going. So, we equip and encourage every believer to:

Connect

Connect with God through worship and with one another in small groups.

Grow

Grow by serving in their God-given ministry, according to their spiritual gifts.

Go

Go into the world and live out and share the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Bridgeway Kids

For more information:

kids@bridgeway.global

We believe that Jesus has a special place in His heart for children. He said, “Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of God belongs to such as these” (Luke 18:16), and it is in light of this truth that we desire to see, care for, and teach each child.

Bridgeway Kids is a growing children’s ministry that serves toddlers and children up to 6th grade. Our classes are organized by age and are currently meeting weekly every Sunday for Bible classes via Zoom.

  • Olive Class: 2-3 year-olds
  • Pine Class: 4-5 year-olds
  • Palm Class: 6-7 year-olds
  • Oak Class: 8-11 year-olds
