Membership

We offer Membership Classes for anyone who wants to become a member of our Bridgeway Church family and begin serving in one of the many opportunities within our church. Membership Classes are offered quarterly, and meet for 4 consecutive weeks after the worship service for about an hour. This is a great opportunity to get to know Pastor Danny as he teaches each class, so we look forward to seeing you at the next session! Please contact Pastor Danny for more information at danny@bridgeway.global.
