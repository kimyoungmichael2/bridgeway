Bridgeway Kids

Welcome to Bridgeway Kids!

We believe that Jesus has a special place in His heart for children. He said, “Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of God belongs to such as these” (Luke 18:16), and it is in light of this truth that we desire to see, care for, and teach each child.

We also believe that parents and guardians are called to be the primary disciple-makers, with the God-given role and authority to bless, teach, and train their children in the love and truth of God. Bridgeway Kids’ vision is to partner with parents and guardians to see this generation live gospel-centered lives, loving God and others. In line with this vision, Bridgeway Kids’ mission is to lead children to love and follow Jesus.

Bridgeway Kids is a growing children’s ministry that serves toddlers and children up to 6th grade. Our classes are organized by age and are currently meeting weekly every Sunday for Bible classes via Zoom.

  • Olive Class: 2-3 year-olds
  • Pine Class: 4-5 year-olds
  • Palm Class: 6-7 year-olds
  • Oak Class: 8-11 year-olds

    • Bridgeway Kids Sunday Service normally occurs at 2:00 in Klein Hall, concurrently with the adult service that gathers in the Chapel. We are prayerfully considering the reopening of our in-person children’s service and classes on a month to month basis, keeping the safety of our kids and families as the utmost priority. Whether face to face or through virtual meetings, we are enjoying being able to spend time with God and with one another as we dig into God’s words together. We are currently using the Gospel Project Kids curriculum as a way to support parents and guardians with sound Biblical teaching and practical resources. Check out our Bridgeway Kids News, which is how we keep parents and guardians informed about Bible lessons we’re learning and important announcements we share.
